You are a primitive uncellular organism in a constantly evolving world. Your task is to devour other cells, intake their sugar, use your energy (ATP) wisely, and evolve new abilities to survive.

Controls:
WASD -> Movement
Esc -> Quit
Q -> Convert sugar to ATP
E -> Convert sugar to membrane health
Space -> Convert all sugar to ATP until upgrade
Left click -> Shoot antibody
Right click -> Release cytokine storm
